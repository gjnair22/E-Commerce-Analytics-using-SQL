# 📊 Business Insights

## Project Overview

This document summarizes the key business insights obtained from analyzing the Brazilian E-Commerce dataset using MySQL.

The analysis focuses on customer behavior, order fulfillment, payment preferences, and product availability to support business decision-making.

---

# 1. Customer Base Analysis

### Business Question

How many unique customers has the business served?

### Result

- Total Unique Customers: **96,096**

### Business Insight

The business has successfully served **96,096 unique customers**, indicating a large customer base and strong market reach.

### Business Recommendation

Focus on customer retention strategies such as loyalty programs and personalized recommendations to encourage repeat purchases.

---

# 2. Order Analysis

### Business Question

How many orders have been placed?

### Result

- Total Orders: **99,441**

### Business Insight

Nearly **100,000 orders** have been processed, demonstrating a high transaction volume suitable for operational and sales trend analysis.

### Business Recommendation

Continue monitoring order growth over time to improve demand forecasting and inventory planning.

---

# 3. Delivery Performance

### Business Question

How many orders were successfully delivered?

### Result

- Delivered Orders: **96,478**
- Delivery Success Rate: **97.02%**

### Business Insight

Approximately **97% of all orders** were successfully delivered, reflecting an efficient order fulfillment and logistics process.

### Business Recommendation

Investigate the remaining unsuccessful deliveries to identify operational bottlenecks and improve customer satisfaction.

---

# 4. Product Category Analysis

### Business Question

How many product categories are available?

### Result

- Total Product Categories: **73**

### Business Insight

The platform offers products across **73 distinct categories**, indicating a diverse product portfolio capable of serving different customer needs.

### Business Recommendation

Analyze category-level sales performance to identify high-performing and underperforming categories for inventory optimization.

---

# 5. Payment Method Analysis

### Business Question

Which payment methods are preferred by customers?

### Result

| Payment Method | Transactions |
|---------------|------------:|
| Credit Card | 76,795 |
| Boleto | 19,784 |
| Voucher | 5,775 |
| Debit Card | 1,529 |
| Not Defined | 3 |

### Business Insight

- **Credit Cards** are the most preferred payment method, accounting for the majority of transactions.
- **Boleto** is the second most commonly used payment option.
- **Debit Card** transactions are relatively low compared to other payment methods.

### Business Recommendation

Continue optimizing the checkout experience for credit card users while encouraging adoption of alternative payment methods through targeted promotions.

---

# Summary

The initial SQL analysis provides valuable insights into:

- Customer base
- Order volume
- Delivery efficiency
- Product diversity
- Customer payment preferences

These insights establish a strong foundation for further analysis, including customer segmentation, product performance, revenue analysis, and advanced SQL reporting.
